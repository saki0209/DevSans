School Admissions

Admission Overview

Welcome to Dev Sanskriti Vidya Mandir! We are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment. We invite you to explore our admission process and discover why our school is the right choice for your child.

Eligibility Criteria

Our school accepts students from various backgrounds. Basic eligibility criteria include:

  • Age: 3 years and above
  • Academic qualifications: Previous school records

Application Process

Steps to Apply

Follow these steps to complete your application:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. There is no any application fee.
  3. Submit required documents.
  4. Participate in the interview.

Important points

Key points to remember:

  • Application Deadline: Before 10 days from admission.
  • After the submission of application form and documents.

Interviews

Interviews help us get to know prospective students better. Prepare by:

  • Understanding the school's mission and values
  • Practicing common interview questions
  • Being yourself and staying relaxed

Supporting Documents

Required Documents

Ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Previous school transcripts
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Proof of age (birth certificate)
  • Identification (passport or national ID)

Submission Guidelines
